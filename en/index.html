MISSION

Many cultural heritages and artifacts are lying dormant storage rooms all over the world (some of which are digitized but underutilized). We have developed a platform that allows anyone, anywhere, at any time, to create and publish virtual exhibitions: Virtualion®. Our mission is to establish a museum for the virtual age that connects people with cultural materials and brings everyone into close contact with cultural properties and works of art.

Our CEO and board of directors have worked with museums to curate number of exhibitions. Our greatest strength is our experience in the field and our extensive network of museum-related contacts.
